Pastor Dr.Anandam Bottu

Dr. Anandam Bottu is known person in South India for bringing pentecost to many parts of South India. He has visited many places from 40 years and planted nearly 85 chruches most of them have multilplied to 4 or 5. Results of his early work is seen now as most of the churches were grown so big.

He worked mostly for bringing change in the tribal(Koya) villages which are close to forest. He has reached many villages with the Word of God and preached them Gospel. His aim to plant atleast 1 small church for 1 tribal villages. He picks interested students from villages and train them in Sharon Bible college and send them to their own villages, which is very friutful.

He also supports koya tribal people with counselling,Bible college, sweing machine training, borewells for safe water, medical camps in rainy season and humanitarin help. He also promotes the value of education among tribal people. He is currently Sponsering 25 tribal children to go to school. His aim to start a school in koya language for tribal people.

He is a Pastor, Leader, Trainer, Speaker, team builder and mainly a man of kind heart. Currently his ministry is located at 320 kms from Hyderabad a famous city in SouthIndia. Sharon society has 18 chruches in tribal villages.

Besides his duties, he is holding different positions in SouthIndian Christian leadership. He is a humble, loving ,always smiling, listens to others, encourages his team to do more for the people. He is a good father makes his children to follow his foot steps. Last but not the least, he believes in team work.
