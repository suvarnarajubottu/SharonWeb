Bible College

Sharon Bible provides bible traning especially for tribal people. 35 tribal students are getting trained every year. After training they go back to thier tribal villages and preach the Gospel to their own group. They not only learn Bible but also how to be a good human being and also moral values.

Church planting

The main objective of the church planting is to reach the Unreached with Gospel, save lives and turn them to Jesus. People in those areas have changed alot, they quit smoking, alcohol and many other bad habits. We send a pastor and his family to that areas who will work for them. Right now we serving with 18 churches, and many more need one

Slum project

This project is to send the tribal children who can not attend school becauses of poverty. Children work in the forest along with their parents without attending school. Many children sit home with out attending school because they can not afford the school fee. They need to walk some miles to attend the school.

Evangelism & Crusades

We conduct village evangelism where we select a village per week and reach the people of the village with the Good News. We distribute tracks, we sing on the roads, we preach in the open places, we talk one-to-one, by projecter we play Jesus movies and use all the possible ways to reach the Unreached. We conduct crusades at diffrent places through out the year.

Water wells

Water wells project gives safe water to families who needs to walk miles to get some drinking water. There are many villages which lack minimum water facilities. We dig bore wells in villages where there is no safe water. This project is ondemand project

Sewing project

Sewing project makes widows and their children self sustainable. We give short term sewing training and a sewing machine for free of cost. They will acquire basic skills in sewing to make some money out of that. Tribal women and girls are most of them that attend this training.
